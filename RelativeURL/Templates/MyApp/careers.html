SRT Solutions L.L.C., a specialty services contractor headquartered in Houston, Texas, provides infrastructure repair services to the midstream oil & gas, power generation and industrial plants in America, Canada, Latin America and the Caribbean. SRT Solutions L.L.C. was founded as a spin off from a very successful specialty industrial maintenance company that sold its mechanical repair services operations in December 2011. Following the sale of their legacy business, the company's founders developed a vision to build an innovative, market-leading, 21st century repair services company.
